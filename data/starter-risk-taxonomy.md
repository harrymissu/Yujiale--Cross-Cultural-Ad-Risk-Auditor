# Starter Risk Taxonomy / 风险分类起步表

This is a starter taxonomy. It is not a cultural taboo database. Add market-specific evidence before using it for real campaigns.

## Risk Dimensions

| Dimension | Examples | Typical Severity |
|---|---|---|
| Language and translation | slang, homophones, literal translation, unnatural slogan | Watch to High |
| Religion and customs | sacred symbols, food restrictions, rituals, modesty | High to Critical |
| Visual symbols | gestures, colors, animals, flags, maps, numbers | Watch to Critical |
| Identity representation | race, gender, age, disability, class, region | Watch to Critical |
| Value conflict | family, authority, modesty, humor, competition | Watch to High |
| Political/geopolitical | borders, flags, historical trauma, national dignity | High to Critical |
| Platform context | meme spread, short-video compression, influencer disclosure | Watch to High |
| Legal/category | health, finance, children, food, cosmetics, supplements | High to Critical |

## Evidence Level

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Verified | Supported by official/platform/academic/local expert source |
| Plausible | Reasonable concern but needs local validation |
| Speculative | Weak signal; mention only as a watch item |

## Recommended Validation Methods

- Native-language copy review
- Local cultural consultant review
- Platform policy review
- Legal/compliance review
- Small local focus group
- A/B test with comment monitoring
- Review of recent local controversies and competitor ads
