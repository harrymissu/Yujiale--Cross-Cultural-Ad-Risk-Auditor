# Sample Report / 示例报告

> This sample is fictional and is only used to demonstrate the output format.

## 1. Executive Summary / 核心结论

- Overall risk score: 58 / 100
- Risk level: High
- Main concern: The slogan and visual concept rely on humor and gesture symbolism that may be misunderstood in the target market.
- Recommendation: Major revision required before launch. Keep the core benefit message, but replace the ambiguous gesture and rewrite the slogan in locally natural language.

## 2. Input Recap / 输入信息复述

- Target market: Example Market A
- Platform: Short-video social media
- Product category: Consumer electronics
- Creative idea: A young user makes a hand gesture after using the product and says a humorous slogan.
- Assumptions: The ad is intended for a broad young-adult audience.

## 3. Risk Map / 风险地图

| Dimension | Risk Level | Evidence / Reason | Confidence | Suggested Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Language and translation | Medium | The slogan uses a direct translation that sounds unnatural. | Medium | Rewrite with local copywriter. |
| Religious and customary risk | Low | No obvious religious element. | Medium | Confirm with local reviewer. |
| Visual symbols | High | The gesture may have unintended meanings in some contexts. | Medium | Replace with product-use scene or neutral icon. |
| Identity and representation | Watch | The character design may be too generic and feel non-local. | Low | Add locally authentic details after research. |
| Platform context | High | Short clips may be shared without context, increasing misreading risk. | Medium | Ensure first 3 seconds explain the product benefit clearly. |

## 4. Key Risk Findings / 重点风险点

### Risk 1: Ambiguous Gesture

- Trigger element: Hand gesture used as the final punchline.
- Why it may be risky: Gesture meanings are highly context-dependent and can be misread across markets.
- Possible audience reaction: Confusion, joking reinterpretation, or negative comments.
- Severity: High
- Confidence: Medium
- How to verify: Ask native reviewers from the target market and test with a small local audience.

## 5. Safer Alternatives / 低风险改编方案

| Alternative | Why safer | Creative value preserved | Remaining risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Replace gesture with product UI animation | Avoids culturally loaded body language | Keeps “instant benefit” message | Low |
| Use a simple thumbs-up only after local validation | More globally recognizable but still context-dependent | Keeps upbeat tone | Medium |
| End with user reaction and on-screen benefit text | Reduces symbol ambiguity | Keeps humor and emotional payoff | Low |

## 6. Local Validation Checklist / 本地验证清单

- [ ] Native-language slogan review
- [ ] Gesture interpretation check
- [ ] Platform policy review
- [ ] 5–10 person local quick test

## 7. Final Recommendation / 最终建议

Major revision required before launch. Keep the product benefit and comedic rhythm, but remove the ambiguous gesture and rewrite the slogan with local copywriting support.
